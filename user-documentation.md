# User Documentation

## App Description

The Cornucopia App is a mobile platform that connects donors with local charities and community organizations in need of goods. Designed to simplify the donation process, Cornucopia allows individuals to discover nearby charities, view their current needs, and schedule item drop-offs.

## Installation

Install Cornucopia App on the iOS or the Google Play store.

## Using the App

Cornucopia offers two distinct user experiences: one for Donors and one for Charity Administrators. Each user type has its own manual with detailed instructions on setup, navigation, and features. Please refer to the section that corresponds to your user type for detailed instructions on setup, navigation, and features.

- **Donor Section**: For individuals looking to discover charities, make donations, and track their impact.
- **Charity Section**: For organizations managing item requests, coordinating with donors, and tracking received donations.

## Bug Reporting

If you encounter any bugs, errors, or unexpected behavior while using Cornucopia, please report them so our team can address them promptly. You can submit bug reports by emailing: azitab@outlook.com. To help us reproduce and fix the issue efficiently, please include the following details in your report:

**1. Description of the Bug**: A clear summary of the problem.
Example: “When I try to schedule a donation, the confirmation page does not appear.”

**2. Steps to Reproduce**: A step-by-step guide showing exactly how to trigger the bug.
Example:

- Log in as a donor
- Navigate to the “Donate” tab
- Select a charity and items
- Schedule a drop-off
- Click “Confirm”

**3. Expected vs. Actual Behavior**

- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened

**4. Device and Environment Details**

- Device type (iPhone, Android phone, tablet)
- Operating system version (e.g., iOS 17.2, Android 13)

**5. Photos & Videos**

- Include screenshots, screen recordings, or error messages if possible

**6. Frequency & Severity**

- How often the bug occurs
- How severe it is

## Donor Manual

### Account Creation

- Open the Cornucopia app.
- Tap Sign Up on the landing page.
- Enter your name, email, and password.
- Tap Create Account.

### Account Settings & Notifications

- Edit your personal information via Profile > Personal Information.
- Set notification preferences in Profile > Notifications.
- Change your password via Profile > Account Settings.

### Finding Charities

- Tap the Donate tab.
- Tap Start Your Search.
- Enter your location or use device location.
- Apply filters for category, urgency, and distance.
- Browse results in list view or map view.
- Tap a charity to see details: mission, reviews, and current needs.
- Save charities to your Favorites for future reference.

### Making a Donation

- Tap Donate on the charity’s page.
- Select items to donate, specifying quantity, size, or condition if applicable.
- Tap Next to schedule your drop-off.
- Choose a date and time from the available slots.
- Review your donation and tap Confirm.

### Managing Donations

- Go to My Donations tab.
- View upcoming donations with details: items, charity, date/time, and location.
- Tap a donation to see drop-off instructions or contact the charity.
- Edit or cancel donations before the scheduled time if needed.
- After completion, donations move to the Past Donations section.

### Reviewing Charities

- Navigate to My Donations and select a completed donation.
- Tap Leave a Review.
- Rate the charity (1–5 stars) and optionally add comments.
- Tap Submit.
